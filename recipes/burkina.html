Burkina / Degue

Description

Brukina (or Burkina) is a popular and nutritious Senegalese and Ghanaian beverage made from ground millet and fermented or pasteurized cow's milk, sometimes mixed with yogurt and sweetened with sugar or condensed milk.

Ingredients

  • Millet Couscous
  • Plain Yogurt
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Whole Milk
  • Condensed Milk

Steps

  1. Wash millet couscous with cold water.

  2. Pour hot water on the millet couscous and cover it to cook it (Should take about 20 to 25 minutes).

    Or You can grind the millet into fine powder and form small millet pebbles. Then steam-cook the pebbles.

  3. Mix sugar, vanilla extract, condensed milk, whole milk, and plain yogurt together in a bowl. Taste and adjust as needed.
  4. Add the cooked millet couscous to the mixture.
